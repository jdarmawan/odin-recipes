CHICKEN WINGSSSSSSS

Baked chicken wings are so easy to make, and they come out of the oven crispy and delicious. There's no need for flour!

Simply bake them in a 400Â°F oven for 40 minutes. And if you want them extra-crispy, leave them in a warm oven for 30-60 more minutes

Ingredients:
  • Fresh chicken wings
  • Butter
  • Spices
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
How to make:
  1. Toss the wings in melted butter (or even just liberally spray them with oil) and sprinkle them with spices.
  2. Arrange them on a rimmed baking sheet fitted with a wire rack.
  3. Bake until fully cooked, well-browned, and wonderfully crispy!
